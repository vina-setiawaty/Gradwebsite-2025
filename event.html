Getting Here

Come find us at SDE4, 8 Architecture Dr, 117356. The NUS DID Graduation Show will be held at Level 5.

By Car

Turn into campus from Clementi Road and enter SDE4. Enter the building from the Level 2 drop off area.

By Train

Alight at Clementi MRT station then take bus 96, 183 or 188 (details below);
Alight at Kent Ridge MRT station then take bus D1, A1, or 95 (details below)

By Bus

There are 3 bus stops nearest to SDE4

Ctrl Lib (16181)

Buses 95, 151, D1, A1, K, BTC

Take a walk down the slope and cross the street to SDE4. Walk up the stairs on the left to enter the building.

Aft Architecture Dr (16199)

Buses 95, 96, 151

Take a short walk down the slope to SDE 4. Walk up the stairs to the left to enter the building.

SDE3 (16149)

Buses 33, 96, 183, 188, E

Turn right and walk to the end of the carpark and enter SDE4.
